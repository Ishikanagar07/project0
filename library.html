Library: A home away from home

The simplest definition of a library: It's a place that houses and lends books to its members. But in this age of digital information, e-books and the internet, is there still a reason to go to the library? The answer is an emphatic "yes." More than just the place where books live, libraries are an integral part of any community. They provide information, resources and a connection to the world at large.
